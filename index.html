The Firefighters Charitable Foundation

A Responsive Redesign of a Non-Profit Website

I helped a nonprofit bring awareness to their mission through the introduction of an updated navigation sytem and reorganization of their website.

ROLE

Project Manager, UX Researcher, and UI Designer within a team of three

TIMELINE

Three Weeks

TOOLS

Miro, Figma, Whimsical, Google Suite, and Zoom

The Problem

The Firefighters Charitable Foundation was intended to provide financial support to people impacted by fire and disaster, prevention programs, and volunteer fire departments. We have observed our website does not provide a local connection for people to get involved, causing users to go to other local organizations. How might we help people connect to their local chapters so they can tangibly see the value of their contributions to others and themselves?

Our Solution

We believe providing local options and highlighting the positive impacts of donations to people who are interested in getting involved will achieve an increase of contributions to the foundation and thus help more people in need.
